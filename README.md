An all-in-one addon to simplify the dungeon experience on WoW WotLK 3.3.5. It automates social interactions and dungeon queuing via a minimap control button.
✨ Features

    🟢 Minimap Button: A visual indicator (Green = ON, Red = OFF) to toggle the addon with one click. Right-click to drag and reposition.

    ⚔️ Auto-Queue: Automatically prompts you to join the Dungeon Finder (LFD) 5 seconds after logging in or leaving an instance.

    👋 Auto-Politeness: Sends a greeting message ("Hello everyone, have a good run!") 10 seconds after entering a dungeon.

    🛡️ Tank Marking: Automatically places a raid icon (Star) on the group's Tank 12 seconds after entering an instance.

    💰 Auto-Loot Roll: Automatically confirms loot rolls when choosing "Greed" to skip annoying pop-up confirmations.

🛠️ Installation

    Download the ZIP archive.

    Extract the contents.

    Place the QuickQueue-WotLK folder into your Interface/AddOns/ directory.

    In-game, click the QQ button on your minimap to manage the addon.

Note: You must be the Group Leader or an Assistant for the Auto-Tank Marking feature to work.
